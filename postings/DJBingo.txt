Job Overview:
We are seeking a talented and energetic personality to join our team. As a weekly DJ Trivia/ DJ BINGO Host, you will be responsible for providing entertainment and music for various events and venues. This is an exciting opportunity for someone who is passionate about music and enjoys creating a fun and engaging atmosphere.
Responsibilities:
-Responsible for having Games run according to company policies on time
-Set up and operate equipment, including sound systems and microphones(all provided to you)
- Interact with guests and keep the crowd engaged, Be personable with players and engage
- Monitor sound levels and adjust as necessary to ensure optimal audio quality
- Setup and breakdown of equipment before and after each game
- Up-to-date with current music trends and popular songs (preferred)
Qualifications:
- Previous experience in a related role is preferred but not required
- Strong knowledge of different music genres, including popular songs from various eras
- Excellent customer service skills to interact with players in a friendly and professional manner
- Ability to speak confidently in public and make announcements during events
- Basic understanding of audio equipment setup and troubleshooting (preferred)
- Ability to lift heavy equipment (speakers, amplifiers, etc.) as needed (50lbs)
- Strong communication skills to effectively deal with any issues that may arise.
If you have a passion for music, enjoy entertaining others, and have the skills required for this role, we would love to hear from you. Apply now to join our dynamic team as a DJ Trivia /DJ BINGO Host
